# Research

Our research interests primarily lie in the field of **computer architecture** with a focus on **memory subsystems**:

- Main memory performance/power/reliability
- Processing-in-memory (PIM) / near-data-processing (NDP)
- Hybrid DRAM-NVM systems
- Memory-based accelerators

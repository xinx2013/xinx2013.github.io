    About

    Click to edit text

    I am a tenure-track assistant professor in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) at the University of Central Florida. I received my Ph.D. from the University of Pittsburgh, where I worked with my advisor Dr. Jun Yang on high-performance memory systems and advanced computer architectures. I received my Master's and Bachelor's degrees from Tsinghua University in 2016 and Lanzhou University in 2013, respectively.

    Get in touch at xin.xin@ucf.edu Office: HEC 317A

    Research

    Click to edit text

    My research interests primarily lie in the field of Computer Architecture with emphasis on High-Performance Memory, Reliable Hardware/Software Systems, and Fully Homomorphic Encryption Accelerators.

    I also have wide-ranging research/working experiences in Electrical and Computer Engineering, encompassing Integrated Circuit (IC) Design and Semiconductor Devices.
